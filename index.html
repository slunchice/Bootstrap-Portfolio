About Me

Elena Guedes is a Math teacher for CMS. She teaches 10th and 11th graders at an academy of technology on Freedom Dr. in Charlotte, NC.

She graduated from Virginia Commonwealth University with a Masters in Educational Leadership. She also holds an undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Mathematics.

Elena loves cats, her two daughter, and her husband Thiago. She speaks fluent Bulgarian, Portuguese, English, Spanish, Russian, and German. Well, maybe not fluent German.
